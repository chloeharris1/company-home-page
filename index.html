Quality Bulldog Puppies and Stud Service

We are a family living in North Central Washington on Osoyoos Lake. I have always loved animals and volunteered at the SPCA as a teenager and then went to work for a vet for many years. We bought our first bulldog in 1997 and I fell so madly in love with the breed that I decided to raise them.


My goal is to breed gorgeous healthy bulldogs bred to the standard that make excellent family pets or show dogs.


I do ask for occasional updates and I am available to help for the life of the puppy. I have made many life time friends through my bullies.

Pups are raised in the bedroom until they are 4 weeks old and then moved to the living room. They are loved cuddled and kissed most of their waking hours. Nothing gives me more pleasure than sitting in my puppy pen snuggling babies.

Puppies are health guaranteed, wormed, microchipped, first vaccs, vet checked and come with a blanket and toy that smell like home, food, health cert, shot record and AKC registration.
